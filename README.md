# Simple Interest Calculator

## Overview
This repository contains a simple interest calculator that computes the interest earned or paid on a principal amount over a specified period of time.

## Formula
The formula used to calculate simple interest is:

**SI = (P × R × T) / 100**

Where:
- **P** = Principal amount (initial sum of money)
- **R** = Rate of interest per annum (in percentage)
- **T** = Time period (in years)
- **SI** = Simple Interest

The total amount (A) after the interest period is:

**A = P + SI**

## Example Calculation
- **Principal (P):** ₹10,000
- **Rate (R):** 5% per annum
- **Time (T):** 3 years

**Calculation:**
SI = (10000 × 5 × 3) / 100 = ₹1,500

**Total Amount:** ₹10,000 + ₹1,500 = ₹11,500

## How to Use
1. Enter the principal amount (P)
2. Enter the rate of interest (R) in percentage
3. Enter the time period (T) in years
4. Click calculate to get the simple interest and total amount
